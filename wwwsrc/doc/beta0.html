Notes for beta testers.

Firefox: not yet. The new module system for JavaScript, upon which I rely, has been slow to show up in Firefox, so you'll have to use one of the other modern browsers: Chrome, Edge, or Safari.

Only the user interface is ready for testing. The coding side of things will be left for a little later. By all means have a look at the code and catalog editors if you like, and let me know of any problems - but I haven't done my own careful pass through these.

I am very interested in bugs, but workability and comprehensibility of the prototype scheme is also a major issue. Do prototypes, as I explain them, make sense to you? What's hard or confusing about making or editing a diagram by these methods?

This is the approach to testing that I would advise.

(1) Register for an account, via "sign in". As you'll see, I'll need to send you a key. Let me know of any hitches in this process.

(2) Go through the tutorial, trying all the suggested things. Anything that could be clarified?
