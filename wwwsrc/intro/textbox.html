Text Boxes

You can use any of the basic shapes as a text box simply by dragging "text" from the catalog into the shape (the shape will highlight when it is ready to receive the text). Thereafter, the text will move around with its box (this remains true when the text is edited to a new value). Furthermore, the box can be replaced by another shape at any time using the "replace" or "replace prototype" topbar actions. Exercise: insert a shaded circle, then drag "text" into it.
