How to Build a Bracket (or Tree of Ancestors)

To enter a new name, just click on the "name" you wish to enter, and then click "Edit Text". If you click any blank space over the bracket, the whole bracket will be selected, and you can resize it, and change its parameters (notably numLevels and textUp). Decide how many levels you want first, then enter names.

To edit the properties of text (such as color or font), select a name. On the right panel two boxes will appear, labeled "Instance" and "Prototype". If you modify properties of "Instance" only the name you have selected will be affected, but modification of properties of the "Prototype" will affect all names. This is explained in greater detail here.

Saving diagrams, and producing them in jpg or svg form (where they can be accessed via URL, or embedded in web pages), are general capabilities of PrototypeJungle. The tutorial page for saving is here. You can deploy all of the diagramming capabilities of PrototypeJungle for your brackets(useful perhaps for adding commentary or arrows). If this is of interest, please peruse the rest of the tutorial.
