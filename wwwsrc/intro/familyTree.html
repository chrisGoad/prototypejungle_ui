How to Build a Family Tree

To get started , click on a parent node to select it. Then, click on Add Child, and note that the expected thing happens. You can add names and pictures for any person via Edit Text, and Add Image. If you select (click on) the child and then Add Partner (left or right) a partner for the child will be added. To add a child of a couple, select the right partner of the couple, then click Add Child. Undo is always available.

You can control the layout of the tree in two ways.

First, you can drag around families by their little red handles. Try it. Notice that all descendants (to whatever generation) follow along. You can drag individuals too. If an individual has a partner he/she can only be dragged horizontally. Finally, in terms of dragging, you can drag the junction point of branching lines up and down, by clicking on those lines, and dragging the little yellow square that appears.

Second, the menu command "Layout Family" automatically chooses positions for every descendant of the family to which the selected person belongs, but leaves ancestors alone. As you will see, the automated layout tools will not do the whole job in complex cases, so some hand layout will be needed (hand layout also allows you to get the look you want).
