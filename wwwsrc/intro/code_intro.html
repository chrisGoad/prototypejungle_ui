Note that when the circles are dragged or resized with the mouse, the arrows are updated accordingly.

If you have clicked on one of the circles, its own properties and those of its prototype are displayed in two boxes in the right-hand panel, where they can be adjusted. Same for the arrows.

A circle can be replaced with, eg, a square by clicking on the "Replace" option in the top bar, and then dragging the square that appears in the right-hand panel over the circle you wish to replace ("Replace prototype" will replace both circles).
