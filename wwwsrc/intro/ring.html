How to Build a Tree

To get started, click on one of the bottom two nodes to select it.

"Add Child" and "Add Sibling" (left or right/above or below) do the expected things. Try them.

The layout of the tree can be controlled in two ways. First, you can drag the nodes of the tree individually. Note that their subtrees follow along. Second, the menu command "Reposition Subtree" automatically chooses positions for every descendant of the selected node.

There are three parameters applying to the whole tree that can be accessed via "Select Kit Root": includeArrows, hSpacing, and vSpacing. hSpacing and vSpacing only have effect when you reposition a subtree.
